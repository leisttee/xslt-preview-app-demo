1.	Printout templates in customer projects
Parameters usable in templates (DTO) are in code .../Synerall/_git/Core/Source/BusinessLogic/Export/DTOs
There can be utility (electricity, heating, gas, water, waste), language (Finnish, Swedish, English, Estonian, Russian etc) or sending channel (post, e-mail, gsm) variations, depending on customer.
List of template types in main branch:
•	Footer of general e-mail messages sent to customers by the company
•	Connection contract
•	Grid agreement
•	Supply agreement and microproduction purchase agreement (in one common template)
•	Supply agreement renewal offer
•	Invoice
•	Invoice payment reminder: 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th
•	Disconnection warning
•	Payment plan
•	Annual consumption report
•	Planned outage start notice
•	Planned outage update notice
•	Planned outage end notice
•	Planned outage cancellation notice
•	Actual outage start notice
•	Actual outage update notice
•	Hourly prices for Spot products
•	Work order
•	Work order notice to customer
•	Meter reading reminder
•	Connection end notice
•	Background contract (not used any more)
